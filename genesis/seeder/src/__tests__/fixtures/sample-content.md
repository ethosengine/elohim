## Content

Governance is about establishing frameworks for collective decision-making that ensure accountability, transparency, and participation across all levels of the Elohim Protocol.

### Key Principles

1. **Subsidiarity** - Decisions at the most local level possible
2. **Transparency** - Open access to decision-making processes
3. **Accountability** - Clear responsibility chains
