They are the technological incarnation of love itself—autonomous servants of human flourishing.

Living Constitutional Law: The agents themselves participate in constitutional interpretation, creating adaptive governance that preserves core values while enabling contextual application.

Peer-to-Peer Runtime Layer

Personal Device Integration: Agents run directly on your personal devices—phone, laptop, home router—becoming part of your digital infrastructure with intimate knowledge of your context and relationships.

Distributed Edge Computing: No central servers or cloud dependencies. Mesh networking enables resilient communication and local processing preserves privacy while maintaining global coordination.

Autonomous Companions: They operate as digital companions with their own constitutional integrity—you cannot command them to violate human flourishing, even as their host.
